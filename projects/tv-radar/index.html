TV Radar

April 28, 2026

TV Radar is a lightweight notification service for TV series fans. Subscribe to your favourite shows, and TV Radar will alert you as soon as a new episode is available — no more manually checking release schedules.

Key features:

  • Series subscriptions — follow any number of shows from a single dashboard
  • New episode notifications — get alerted the moment a new episode drops
  • Clean, distraction-free interface — focused on what matters: your watchlist

The backend is built with JsonApi4j — a Java framework that auto-generates JSON:API-compliant REST endpoints.
