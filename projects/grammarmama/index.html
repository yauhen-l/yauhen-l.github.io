GrammarMama

April 4, 2026

GrammarMama is an AI-powered grammar checker and translator that instantly corrects text, explains every mistake, and translates between languages — no signup required.

Paste your text, and it returns a fully corrected version along with a structured list of mistakes with clear explanations. You can also translate the result into another language in the same step.

Key features:

  • No signup — zero friction, just paste and correct
  • Full corrected text shown immediately, not just inline suggestions
  • Translation — translate to and from English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, and Russian
  • Structured mistake list with educational explanations for each error
  • Mixed-language detection — foreign words are identified and translated into the target language
  • Multilingual — verify grammar in English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Russian, and more

The tool uses Claude API to analyze and correct text input.
