GrammarMama

April 4, 2026

GrammarMama is an AI-powered adaptive language tutor that meets you where you are and grows with you. It assesses your current proficiency from A0 to C2, learns from your mistakes and vocabulary gaps, and generates personalized daily exercises tailored to your own goals — written in your own words.

Tell GrammarMama what you want to achieve (“I want to hold business conversations in German” or “I’m preparing for IELTS”), and it builds a learning path around that goal. Each session adapts to your progress: exercises increase in complexity as your skills improve, and recurring weak spots get extra attention until they stick.

Key features:

  • Level assessment — evaluated against the A0–C2 CEFR scale from your very first interaction
  • Goal-driven learning — describe your goal in free text; the tutor shapes exercises around it
  • Daily exercises — fresh practice generated each day with gradually increasing complexity
  • Mistake tracking — errors are remembered across sessions so weak areas get targeted reinforcement
  • Vocabulary tracking — new and difficult words are logged and recycled into future exercises
  • Multilingual — supports English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Russian, and more

The tool uses Claude API to power assessment, exercise generation, and adaptive feedback.
